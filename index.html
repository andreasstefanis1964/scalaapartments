About Scala Apartments

located in Kastraki on the island Naxos in Cyclades

The name "Scala" comes from the Venetians, who dominated the island centuries ago. They named the place "Scala" because from here began the "climbing" for anyone wanting to visit the villages of the island. So in our desire to maintain the place's name we named it "Scala". For the past 25 years we give all our love and passion to become a small oasis of holiday in the area of ​​Kastraki.

It consists of ten separate studios-apartments in four different buildings, all designed by following the Cycladic architecture.
Our complex is located in field of 12.000m2, full of trees, vines and flowers.
All the buildings are surrounded by small stone paths and gardens with a variety of local flowers.
